About Me

I'm a Gulf Coast native, born and raised in Mississippi, with a passion for clinical health informatics. I hold a Bachelor of Interdisciplinary Studies in Clinical Health Informatics from Georgia State University, where I graduated in 2017.

My educational journey equipped me with in-depth knowledge in areas such as advanced medical terminology, electronic health records, database management, project management, leadership, healthcare security and privacy, healthcare policy and law, statistics, healthcare economics, and more.

Currently, I'm further honing my skills as a Full Stack Web Developer at the University of Central Florida (UCF), where I'm enrolled in a comprehensive program covering HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Express.js, SQL, React, and more. This program at UCF complements my background in health informatics and enhances my abilities in web development.
